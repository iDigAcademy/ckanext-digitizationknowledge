    Privacy Policy

    This document explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. For information about who we are, see the About Us section in our Terms of Use.

    Definitions

    Services: The <Digitization Knowledge> web application and its contents, including the application programming interface (API).

    User: Anyone who accesses or uses the Services.

    Data: Structured metadata stored in records related to digitization resources.

    Information We Collect

    We collect personal data (such as name, email address, and password) only for account creation and authentication. We do not track or store your search history.

    How We Use Your Information

    Personal data is used solely for:

    • Creating and managing your user account
    • Authenticating your identity when you log in
    • Communicating with you about the Services

    Data Sharing and AI

    We do not sell or disclose user data to third parties. Personal data and user-generated content (such as comments) will not be used to train artificial intelligence models.

    However, we cannot prevent unauthorized third parties from scraping publicly accessible content. For our policies on automated access, see our Terms of Use.

    How Your Data Is Stored

    User registration is facilitated via Google Forms. Information submitted during registration is stored on Google's platform and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Only authorized team members have access to this data.

    The Services are hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS). Data is processed and stored in accordance with AWS's privacy practices.

    The application uses local session storage in your browser to remember your preferences on search forms. This information is not transmitted to our servers.
